## Raw Notes
Most engaged tester. High energy, reading aloud, celebrating correct answers. Validated core concept strongly. Two emphatic critiques: (1) needs way more visuals — "dopamine's cooked now," attention spans are 6 seconds; (2) explanation tone feels like school assignment — needs to sound like "a really social friend" e.g. "Yo, I totally see why you'd say that, actually…" instead of formal teacher voice. Suggested testing on Pierre (less socially skilled person) for better validation signal. "If this worked on Pierre, you're making money."

## Top 3 Takeaways
1. **Tone is wrong** — explanations sound like a school assignment, need to sound like a social friend
2. **More visuals, less words** — TikTok/IG generation needs visual-first, 6-second attention spans
3. **Core concept strongly validated** — "huge demand" as socializing skills decline generationally
