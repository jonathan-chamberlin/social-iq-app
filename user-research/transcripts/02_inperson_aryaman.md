## Raw Notes
Very detailed, constructive feedback. Key UX issues: (1) after onboarding, should jump straight into lesson, not show full lesson list — it's intimidating; (2) needs progress bar within lessons; (3) needs summary page at end showing what you learned; (4) needs time commitment question like Duolingo ("how much time per day"); (5) add streaks for daily engagement; (6) add caricatures/characters reacting to situations. Also flagged that scenarios need life-stage context — a student won't relate to freelancing scenarios. Onboarding "biggest problem" options didn't fit him — same issue as Nishanth.

## Top 3 Takeaways
1. **Onboarding → lesson should be immediate** — don't show intimidating lesson list, jump straight to first lesson with progress bar
2. **Add life-stage/age context to onboarding** — scenarios must match the user's world (student vs. professional)
3. **End-of-lesson summary is missing** — users don't know what they just learned; need a recap before "back to lessons"
