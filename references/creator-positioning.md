# Social IQ — Creator Positioning

**Status:** Locked
**Last updated:** 2026-04-26

## Positioning (one line)

I'm a guy who studies social dynamics and explains what men actually do and why.

## Operating Model

Hussey operating model: visibly desirable on camera but never mention it. The visible layer does silent work; the spoken layer is pure observation.

## Pillar

Single pillar: decode male behavior and dating-adjacent social dynamics for women 18–25.

## Core Principle

The protagonist is the pattern, never me.

- Never reference my own attractiveness.
- Never reference my own dating history.
- Visible layer (appearance, presence, framing) carries the implicit credibility.
- Spoken layer stays observational — about the pattern, not about me.

## Why This Works

- Removes the "fuckboy explaining women" failure mode that kills male dating creators.
- Keeps the audience focused on the insight, not the source.
- Visible desirability is signal without being claim — it can't be argued with, only perceived.
- Compounding: every video reinforces "this guy notices things" rather than "this guy wants you to know he's hot."

## Decision Filter

Before posting anything, check:

1. Is the protagonist the pattern, or did I become the protagonist?
2. Did I reference my own attractiveness or dating life, even obliquely?
3. Is the spoken layer pure observation?
4. Would a 22-year-old woman watching this learn something about men, or learn something about me?

If any answer fails, rewrite or kill it.
