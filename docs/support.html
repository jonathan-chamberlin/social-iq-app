Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my subscription?

Open the app, go to the paywall screen, and tap "Restore Purchases." Your subscription is tied to your Apple ID and will restore automatically.

How do I delete my account?

Email us at the address below with the subject line "Delete My Account" and we will remove your account and all associated data within 48 hours.

The app is crashing or not loading.

Make sure you are running the latest version from the App Store. If the issue persists, try deleting and reinstalling the app, then sign in again with Apple.
