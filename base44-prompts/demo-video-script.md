# Social IQ — Demo Video Script

3-5 minutes. Screen record the app while doing voiceover. Record in one take if possible, edit later.

---

## HOOK (0:00 - 0:30)

**[Screen: nothing yet, or a title card with "Social IQ"]**

"Most people have had this moment. Someone you care about is going through something, and you say the wrong thing. You meant well. But the conversation dies, they pull back, and you're sitting there thinking 'what did I do wrong?'

The advice out there is useless. 'Just be yourself.' 'Be a good listener.' Cool. But what does that actually mean when your coworker is venting about getting passed over for a promotion and you have five seconds to respond?"

## THE PRODUCT (0:30 - 1:00)

**[Screen: show the app home screen briefly]**

"Social IQ is a social intelligence trainer. It teaches you exactly what to say in real situations by breaking every interaction into three steps: READ the person's emotional state, THINK about what they need from you, then SPEAK with the right words.

But here's what makes it different. It connects to your calendar. It looks at what's on your schedule this week, and it recommends practice scenarios for the actual conversations you're about to have."

## DEMO WALKTHROUGH (1:00 - 3:30)

### Onboarding (1:00 - 1:30)

**[Screen: start the app from the beginning, go through onboarding]**

"So you open the app and it asks you three questions. Where you're at socially, what's hard for you, and what success looks like."

**[Click through the three questions, pick answers naturally]**

"I picked 'I'm social at work but struggle outside of it,' 'I never know what to say,' and 'being someone people enjoy talking to.'"

**[Show the analyzing screen, then the result]**

"It analyzes your answers and tells you where to start. Mine says my biggest skill gap is reading emotional states. Most people say the wrong thing because they don't notice what the other person is feeling."

### Calendar (1:30 - 2:00)

**[Screen: calendar connect page]**

"Then it asks to connect your calendar."

**[Click Google Calendar button, show the scanning animation]**

"It scans your week and pulls out situations where social skills actually matter. I've got lunch with a coworker tomorrow, a gym session Wednesday, and a group dinner Friday. Each one has a matched scenario I can practice before I go."

**[Show the home screen with the three cards]**

"Tomorrow I have lunch with Alex. And the app knows Alex just got passed over for a promotion. So it's recommending I practice this conversation before it happens."

### The Scenario (2:00 - 3:30)

**[Click "Practice now" on The Frustrated Coworker]**

"Here's Alex."

**[Show the image and scenario text]**

"He sits down and says 'I honestly don't even know why I try anymore.' So step one, READ. What state is he in?"

**[Show the four options]**

"Most people pick angry. But look at what he's actually saying. He's not blaming anyone. He's questioning whether effort even matters. That's frustration."

**[Pick the correct answer, show green explanation, read a line from it, click Next]**

"Step two, THINK. Based on his state, what does he need? Advice? Distraction? Validation?"

**[Show options]**

"Validation feels right. But the app teaches you something better. Label. Name exactly what he's feeling out loud. When you put a word on someone's emotion, their brain actually calms down. It's neurological. You're proving you see what's happening inside them."

**[Pick correct answer, show green explanation, click Next]**

"Step three, SPEAK. What do you actually say?"

**[Show options, pick correct answer]**

"'It seems like you feel like none of that work even mattered.' That's a label. You named the exact feeling he couldn't put into words. When someone hears their own emotion named accurately, their guard drops. They feel seen. And then the real conversation starts."

**[Show green explanation]**

"And every answer, right or wrong, gets an explanation. If you pick 'at least you still have a job,' it tells you exactly why Alex will shut down after that. You learn from every choice."

## CLOSE (3:30 - 4:00)

**[Screen: results page, or back to home screen]**

"Social IQ is personalized intelligence for the conversations that actually matter in your life. It reads your schedule, recommends what to practice, and teaches you exactly why certain responses work and others don't.

I built this because I started out terrible socially. But I'm also a systems thinker, so I was able to break down exactly what works and why. The problem with most social skills advice is it's vague. This app makes it concrete. One situation, three steps, clear feedback.

Thanks for watching."

---

## PRODUCTION NOTES

- Screen record the app on your phone or browser (whichever looks better)
- Record voiceover separately if your mic picks up clicking sounds
- Don't rush the scenario section. Let the explanations breathe for a second before clicking Next
- If you mess up a line, keep going. Cut it in post. One continuous demo flow matters more than perfect delivery
- Total target: 3:30 to 4:00. The script above reads slightly long so you have room to cut
