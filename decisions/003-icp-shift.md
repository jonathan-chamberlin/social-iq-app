# Decision 003: ICP Shift — Self-Improvement Guy, Not Introverted Gamer

**Date:** 2026-03-28
**Status:** Decided

---

## Context

The original ICP was: nerds, awkward, isolated, potentially neurodivergent, introverts, game and anime enjoyers.

At a hackathon, two independent founders gave the same feedback unprompted:
- **Ritwik** (CBO at Fragile): lean into charisma and reading people, target the self-improvement niche
- **Kelly Palmer** (Product Design Lead at Eight Sleep): same direction, unprompted

When two unrelated people converge on the same correction, that's signal.

## Decision

**New ICP: The Self-Improvement Guy**
- Male, 18-28
- Reads content about charisma, influence, social dynamics, human nature
- Adjacent to looksmaxxing / self-improvement trend but wants the SOCIAL side, not just physical
- Aspirational references: 48 Laws of Power, How to Win Friends and Influence People, Chris Voss, body language content, networking/career content
- Platforms: TikTok, YouTube (self-improvement creators), Reddit (r/socialskills, r/selfimprovement, r/confidence)
- Core pain: knows social skills matter for career, dating, and life but the advice is vague ("just be confident"). Wants a SYSTEM he can practice

**NOT:** anime nerd / gamer introvert archetype. NOT corporate L&D. NOT therapy-adjacent.

## What Changes

- **Onboarding framing**: career networking, dating confidence, reading people, commanding a room. Language like "level up", "unlock", "master". Not "do you feel anxious in social situations."
- **Scare screen**: missed promotions, girls who lost interest, friends who stopped inviting you, being forgettable. Not anxiety/loneliness framing.
- **Ren's tone**: "here's the move most guys miss" not "here's why that didn't land." Charisma coach breaking down film, not therapist coaching a patient.
- **Brand aesthetic**: clean, dark, masculine, confident. Not gamified/cute.
- **Marketing angles**: charisma, reading people, influence, social dynamics. Not emotional intelligence or communication skills (too soft).

## What Stays the Same

- Core mechanic: READ → THINK → SPEAK scenario quizzes with explanations
- Primary action: read a scenario, pick a response, get a score + explanation
- The Social IQ Loop framework
- Ren as mascot (personality stays, framing shifts)
- Calendar integration concept
- Challenge share viral mechanic
