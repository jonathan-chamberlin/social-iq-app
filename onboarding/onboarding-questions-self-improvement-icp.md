# Onboarding Questions (Self-Improvement/Goal-Oriented ICP)

These questions are written for the current ICP: guys who are already social but want to level up their charisma, influence, and ability to read people.

Replace the onboarding questions and answers with the following. Use these exact texts. Do not rephrase.

Screen 1 — "where are you at socially?"
Options (single select, large tappable cards):
- "I'm solid one-on-one but want to own group settings"
- "I can talk to anyone but conversations stay surface-level"
- "I'm social but I'm not the person people remember"
- "I'm good with friends but freeze with new people or dates"

Screen 2 — "what do you want to get better at?"
Options (single select, large tappable cards):
- "Reading what people actually mean, not just what they say"
- "Knowing exactly what to say in high-pressure moments"
- "Making people feel something when they talk to me"
- "Steering conversations instead of just reacting"
- "Telling stories that make people stop and listen"

Screen 3 — "what does winning look like?"
Options (single select, large tappable cards):
- "Being the person people want at every table"
- "Walking into any room and feeling like I belong"
- "Having people reach out to ME instead of the other way around"
- "Turning strangers into real connections fast"

Do not change anything else. Only replace the onboarding question screens.
